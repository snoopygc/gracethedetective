Protect Yourself from Cyber-Crime

Use Strong Passwords

Create complex passwords using a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Use a unique password for each account.

Keep Software Updated

Regularly update your operating system, browsers, and applications to patch security vulnerabilities.

Be Wary of Phishing

Don't click on suspicious links or download attachments from unknown sources. Verify the sender's identity.
